The object is a 3D plus sign.

I. Convert from local coordinates to eye coordinates through scaling and translation.
II. Modified-Phong lighting model. Ambient light is red.
III. Perspective projection through the perspective function.
IV. keyDownHandler function sets the axis and rotation direction based on the arrow keys pressed.
    If no keys are being pressed, the object does not rotate.
